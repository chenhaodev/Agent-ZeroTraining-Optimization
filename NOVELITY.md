The query sounds like a reinforcement‑learning or multi‑agent “Agent Zero” training question, but the retrieved papers are about medical AI and VR training, not agent‑based optimization. They do, however, illustrate several optimization ideas that loosely parallel training‑strategy design.

Conclusion  结论

No paper here addresses a specific “Agent Zero” algorithm, but they converge on learnable principles: define objectives precisely, iteratively refine prompts/commands, use voting or ensembles for robustness, match strategy (zero‑shot, few‑shot, CoT) to task type, and regularize or fuse models to control overfitting and boost performance. For concrete “Agent Zero” training methods, a search directly in RL/multi‑agent optimization literature would be needed.
这里没有论文专门针对“零号代理”算法，但它们都汇聚在可学习的原则上：精确定义目标，迭代优化提示/命令，使用投票或集成来提高鲁棒性，根据任务类型匹配策略（零样本、少样本、CoT），以及正则化或融合模型以控制过拟合并提升性能。对于具体的“零号代理”训练方法，需要在 RL/多智能体优化文献中进行直接搜索。
